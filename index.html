The Problem

Third-Party AI APIs Create Enterprise Risk

External AI APIs can introduce privacy concerns, compliance challenges, vendor lock-in, latency, downtime risk, and unpredictable costs. For enterprises handling sensitive data, AI must be secure, controlled, and self-contained.

01

Data Privacy Risk

Sensitive prompts and records leave your controlled environment.

02

Vendor Lock-In

Roadmap and model availability are controlled by external providers.

03

Unpredictable Costs

Usage-based pricing scales with traffic, not your budget strategy.

04

Compliance Complexity

Proving data residency, auditability, and controls gets harder with external calls.
